Modelo publicado: Dixon-Coles completo penalizado.

Decisión científica

Publish the parsimonious coherent score model unless the ensemble demonstrates a material and statistically supported World-Cup-specific Log Loss improvement over the score-model family. The 95% bootstrap intervals for ensemble improvement versus Poisson and Dixon-Coles cross zero; therefore the ensemble remains a research comparator and is not the public predictor.

Dixon-Coles is retained instead of pure Poisson because it is a one-parameter low-score extension with comparable transport metrics and yields a coherent joint score distribution. Poisson remains the mandatory sensitivity baseline.
